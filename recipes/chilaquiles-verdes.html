Chilaquiles Verdes

Description

Chilaquiles Verdes is a very delicious mexican recipe wich is commonly made for breakfast.

Try this awesome and easy recipe following these steps.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of green tomatoes
  • 1 serrano chile
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Fried tortillas
  • Chicken soup cube

Steps

  1. Boil the green tomatoes, the serrano chile and garlic with a 1 1/4 cup of water for 25-30 minutes with low fire.
  2. Mix the boiled Ingredients in the blender, add a tablespoon of of oil to a cooking pot and put the sauce we have made with a little bit of salt and the chiken soup cube.
  3. Leave it with low fire and taste it after 10-15 minutes. If more salt is needed, add it and wait 5 minutes before turn off the stove.
  4. Put the fried tortillas in a plate, put the sauce over them and add sour cream, chopped onion or chicken as you like.
